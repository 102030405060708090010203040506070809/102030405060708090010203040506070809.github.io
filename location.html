Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I park?

We offer free parking in the lot adjacent to our building. Street parking is also available nearby.

Is your location wheelchair accessible?

Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible, including ramps and restrooms.

Do you take reservations?

Yes, we accept reservations. You can book a table online through our Dine With Us page.

Are pets allowed?

We allow pets in our outdoor seating area. Service animals are welcome inside the restaurant.

What public transport options are available?

Our location is easily accessible by bus, with stops at Vegan Street and Fresh Town Square. Check your local transit schedule for more details.
